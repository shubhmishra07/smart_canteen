About Our System

Smart Canteen addresses the common problem of long queues and waiting times in college cafeterias. Our innovative pre-ordering system allows students and staff to plan their meals in advance, reducing congestion and improving the overall dining experience.

With real-time order tracking and cashless transactions, we've transformed the traditional canteen experience into a modern, efficient service that saves valuable time for busy students and staff members.
